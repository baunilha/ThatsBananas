A Little More

Who knew bananas could be so fascinating? It's been a part of popular culture since the late 1800s and popular ever since it was introduced to Americans. It's hard to imagine something so normal and mundane as bananas having a fragile existence in the world but we hope that That's Bananas introduced you to thinking about the delicate future of this fruit and the reasons for its demise.

We hope that you pursue your banana interests and discover more about how its cultivation has developed and impacted our world, for better or for worse.
